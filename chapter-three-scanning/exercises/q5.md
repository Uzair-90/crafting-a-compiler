### Question No. 5

Write a regular expression that deﬁnes a C-like, ﬁxed-decimal literal
with no superﬂuous leading or trailing zeros. That is, 0.0, 123.01, and
123005.0 are legal, but 00.0, 001.000, and 002345.1000 are illegal.

### Solution

A good expression would be:

```C
(0 | [1-9][0-9]*) "." (0 | [0-9]*[1-9])
```
